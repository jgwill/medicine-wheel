**Title:** Internal Reflective Loops for Coherence Preservation in Human-AI Narrative Generation

**Abstract:**
This paper investigates the implementation of internal reflective loops within a generative storytelling agent, enabling the system to evaluate its narrative outputs against proximal elements of an encompassing knowledge graph. Conceptualized not as isolated textual refinement but as a relational coherence mechanism, this process facilitates the maintenance of conceptual continuity, structural integrity, and interpretive alignment across distributed socio-technical architectures. By iteratively assessing generated content in light of adjacent conceptual structures, the agent proposes revisions that sustain narrative fidelity to the broader systemic context, thereby addressing a core challenge in human-AI co-creative systems: the preservation of meaning amidst generative expansion. The approach yields a situated methodology for narrative maintenance, with implications for knowledge representation, agentic interpretability, and reflexive design practices in artificial intelligence research.
